About CourseManager

CourseManager is a focused platform designed to simplify course discovery, scheduling, and communication for educational institutions. We deliver a reliable, secure, and easy-to-use experience that helps instructors publish courses and students plan their academic path.

Our mission

Our mission is to make academic planning straightforward and dependable. We prioritize clarity, performance, and data security so users can concentrate on teaching and learning rather than managing tools.

Clarity

Interfaces and workflows designed for quick comprehension and minimal friction.

Reliability

Robust behavior and predictable performance across devices and network conditions.

Security

Practical safeguards for user data and communications, with sensible defaults and clear controls.

What we build

  • Course catalog — searchable listings with clear descriptions and prerequisites.
  • Student schedules — tools to add, remove, and visualize semester plans.
  • Instructor tools — simple course creation and roster management.
  • Contact and support — integrated messaging and email delivery for timely assistance.

Get involved

If you have feedback, need support, or want to suggest a feature, we welcome your input. Clear, actionable feedback helps us improve the product quickly.
